Accommodations in Hamilton

On Campus

The LOC has 30 rooms blocked out for accommodations on campus at McMaster for CAD$90 per night, which can be booked on the registration form. These rooms are first-come-first-serve, and are intended for students and postdocs as a cheaper option.
