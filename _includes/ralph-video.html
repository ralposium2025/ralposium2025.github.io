From Stars to Life: Past, Present and Future

Recording of the Conference Finale


"I have been extremely fortunate in my career to work with my students, postdocs, and many collaborators on a number of fundamental problems in star and planet formation and on the physical and chemical conditions leading to the origin of life. My current and former students and postdocs organized a wonderful conference, held at McMaster University on June 25-27, 2025, to celebrate and honour my contributions to science and scientific life over a 45 year (and counting) career. In this special conference talk, I give a personal perspective on my scientific and personal journey through this fascinating landscape." - Ralph E. Pudritz, July 2025
